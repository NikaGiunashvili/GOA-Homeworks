1. Omlet


  • Whisk 2–3 eggs with salt and pepper.
  • Heat butter in a pan over medium heat
  • Pour in the eggs and let cook without stirring.
  • When mostly set, add cheese, veggies, or meats if desired.
  • Fold the omelet in half and cook another 30 seconds
  • Slide onto a plate and serve!


    • 2. Scrambled Eggs

  • 2–3 eggs
  • Salt & pepper
  • 1 tbsp milk or cream (optional, for fluffiness)
  • Butter or oil


    • 3. Boiled Eggs

  • Place eggs in a pot and cover with cold water.
  • Bring to a boil.
  • When boiling, reduce to a simmer and cook: Soft-boiled: 6–7 minutes Medium: 8–9 minutes Hard-boiled: 10–12 minutes
  • Transfer to ice water for a few minutes to stop cooking and make peeling easier.
