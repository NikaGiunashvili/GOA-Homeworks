Lions (Panthera leo) are large, social cats known for their prominent manes in males and their group living in "prides"

Tigers are the largest members of the cat family, known for their distinct orange fur and black stripes, which provide camouflage in their native Asian habitats.

Zebras are African equines known for their black and white stripes, social herd behavior, and herbivorous diet of grass.

Bison are the largest land mammals in North America, known for their shaggy hair, large hump, and distinctive horns.

Giraffes are the world's tallest mammals, easily identified by their long necks and legs, and spotted coats

The term "desert fox" most commonly refers to the fennec fox, a small, nocturnal canid native to the Sahara and other North African deserts, known for its large ears that regulate body temperature and their excellent hearing for finding prey beneath the sand
